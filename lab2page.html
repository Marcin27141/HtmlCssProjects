• About the .NetLabBirdEncyclopedia® •

«Wherever there are birds, there is hope» Mehmet Murat Ildan

"A bird does not sing because it has an answer, it sings because it has a song" Maya Angelou

.NetLabBirdEncyclopedia is a labor of love created on October 11th 2023 to share our passion for the magnificent avian world with enthusiasts and curious minds alike.

Our goals are:

  1. To share our passion for birds
  2. To spread knowledge about avian world
  3. To practise HTML and CSS skills
Our goals are not:
  • To thoroughly describe each and every bird species
  • To suggest that birds are superior to cats
  • To earn any money by donations

Most important terms to know:

Plummage
A bird's feathers collectively
Keel
A part of bird's anatomy providing a leverage for flight
Endemism
A state of being unique to a defined geographic location (e.g. emu in Australia)
